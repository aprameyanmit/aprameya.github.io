About the Fest

MCA Department

The Department of Master of Computer Applications was founded with the Vision of creating global professionals who are technically ready for the industry. This is a two-year professional postgraduate program with an intake of 120, for individuals who want to master an advanced computer programming language and gain further insight into computer application development. This curriculum combines information from theory and practice.

Aprameya

Aprameya Is The Annual Extravaganza Orchestrated By The Department of MCA At Nitte Meenakshi Institute Of Technology, Bengaluru. This Flagship Technical Fest Is More Than Just An Event, It's An Immersive Experience That Transcends Boundaries. Aprameya Invites Participants To Explore The Interconnected World Of Technology, Challenge Their Limits, And Showcase Their Technical Prowess. Dive Into A Realm Of Innovation, Competition, And Excellence, And Be Part Of Something Extraordinary!.

General Event Rules

  • The Overall Championship will be determined based on the total points earned from all events and participation count.
  • Participants must carry their valid College ID card; it is mandatory for entry.
  • Registration fees are strictly non-refundable.
  • Any kind of misconduct or misbehavior will result in immediate disqualification of the participant/team.
  • Event-specific instructions will be provided by the Event Coordinator before the commencement of the event.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • The institution will not be responsible for the loss of any personal valuables.
  • An individual participant cannot register for multiple events simultaneously.
